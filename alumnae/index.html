About Alumnae

Government College Women University Faisalabad (GCWUF), situated at the heart of the city Faisalabad, was established in 1934 and was upgraded to the current status in January 2013. Since its inception, it has been consistently striving to empower female students with educational skills by offering BS, M.A, MSc, MS/M.Phil and PhD Degree Programs in 25 disciplines.
